Baked Potato

Description

This is a delicious cheap dinner, consisting of a potato baked until crispy in the oven. It can accommodate a wide cariety of delicious toppings according to preference

Ingredients

  • a baking potato
  • sea salt
  • baking tray
  • butter
  • plate
  • knife
  • toppings of your choice, for example, tuna mayo, baked beans, cheese, chilli con carne

Instructions

  1. preheat oven to 220 degrees celsius
  2. wash baking potato
  3. prick potato all over with fork
  4. rub a pinch of salt into the potato
  5. place the potato in the oven for an hour and a half
  6. remove potato, put on plate and cut open with knife
  7. apply butter inside hot potato, it will melt
  8. apply other toppings of choice
  9. eat potato
