Pasta

Description

You can buy pasta from the supermarket. In order to cook it, you must complete the following steps:

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • metal pan
  • water
  • bowl

Instructions

  1. boil water in the pan
  2. when the water begins to boil, add the pasta into the pan
  3. wait for 15 minutes
  4. remove the pasta from the water and place into bowl
  5. eat pasta
