Bolognese Sauce

Description

This is a thick meaty rich sauce which can be put with the pasta and\/or baked potato dishes I have detailed elsewhere on this website.

Ingredients

  • minced beef
  • tinned tomatoes
  • chopped onion
  • chopped garlic
  • chopped carrots
  • ketchup
  • worcestershire sauce
  • tabasco sauce
  • sea salt and pepper
  • big saucepan
  • olive oil

Instructions

  1. put saucepan on the stove and brown meat in olive oil
  2. add in the onion and garlic and cook for 5 more mins
  3. add tinned tomatoes, carrots, and sauces
  4. mix together and simmer for an hour, adding more water if going dry
  5. taste and season to taste
  6. add to potato or pasta
  7. eat bolognese
